Sunbursts are a very popular design trend within web and graphic design. A sunburst represents sun rays (or beams) exploding from a source point. They're a Web 2.0 trend but they tend to work well with other graphical elements.

This post looks at <strong>how to create your own sunbursts</strong>, using existing <strong>Photoshop shapes and brushes</strong> and I've showcased <strong>15 beautiful websites that include the sunburst effect</strong> in some shape or form.
